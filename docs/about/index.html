1. Brief Introduction

I’m a freelance FEA (Finite Element Analysis) specialist focused on structural analysis and simulation-driven design optimization.

I help engineers evaluate structural performance and improve designs using reliable, physics-based modeling—without relying on expensive commercial software.

2. My Background (Story Arc)

  • Mechanical Engineering background with a focus on CAE
  • Master’s-level research in FEM-based analysis and optimization
  • Experience building custom Python workflows for simulation and design improvement
  • Transition from academic research to practical engineering problem-solving

Through years of working with FEM, I developed a strong ability to interpret physical behavior and translate it into efficient simulation models.
Now, I focus on applying that skill to real-world design and optimization problems.

3. My Strengths

  • Structural FEA (stress, deformation, stability analysis)
  • Simulation-based design optimization (BESO, iterative improvement workflows)
  • Custom automation using Python for faster and more consistent results
  • Efficient model setup based on strong spatial intuition
  • Open-source CAE tools (PrePoMax, CalculiX)

I prioritize clarity, efficiency, and practical results—delivering insights that directly support design decisions.

4. My Current Goals

  • Building a freelance career focused on high-value FEA and design optimization work
  • Providing cost-effective simulation solutions using open-source tools
  • Collaborating on engineering problems where structural performance matters

Feel free to explore my work—or reach out if you need help with structural analysis or design optimization.
